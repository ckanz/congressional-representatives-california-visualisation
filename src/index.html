US Congress Members & Bills Force Cluster Layout

This project displays all members of the US congress and the 100 most recent bills as node in a force cluster layout. Bills are connected to the member who introduced the bill.

The size of the nodes of congress members relate to the member's seniority. Sizes of bill nodes indicates the amount of cosponsors a bill has.

The arc inside a member node indicates how many votes the member attended (in precent). The border colours around the bill nodes shows the ditribution of party affilitation of the cosponsors.

You can click on a node to be redirected to the website of the member/bill.

You can use the browser's search feature to highlight text of members or bills.
